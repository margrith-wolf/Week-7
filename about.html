About Me!

Hello! My name is Margrith Wolf and I am an aspiring elite off-road cyclist. Unlike most professionals, I didn't pick up bike racing from a young age, or find NICA, or any other development team. I'm almost 28 years old and probably considered "old" to be starting my run at a professional career.

I'm not new to sports though! I ran triathlon for the University of Oregon my freshman year, before finding the sports of CrossFit and Olympic Weightlifting, competing for nearly eight years at an elite national level.

2022 marked my first year of competitive bike racing and I competed in large well-known races such as The Belgian Waffle Ride, Rebecca's Private Idaho and Spirit World 100.

My commitment to this sport is absolute and at the ripe old age of (almost) 28, I have my sights set on joining the elite ranks of off-road cylcing.
